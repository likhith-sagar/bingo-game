How to play?
  1. Enter username and click on play.
  2. Now, fill the box either manually or use Randomize. Use Reset to empty the box incase you want to fill it again. Once the box is filled, you are good to go.
  3. Share My Id with friends, so they can send request to you and play together.
  4. Type others' Id and click Join to send request.
  5. Click on Match to play with a random player online. You may have to wait until any player is available.

  6. In a match, mark your number within the time. If time runs out, game marks a random number on your behalf.
  7. Completion of a row, column or a diagonal provides one point. Score five points to win.
  8. Click on Leave button to exit from match, after finish.
  9. Click on Play Again button to play again. You need to fill box after the click and then click again to join the match. Button will disappear if player has left the match.
